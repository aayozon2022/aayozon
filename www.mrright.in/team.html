    Who's crazy enough to do this?

    You put a bunch of technopreneurs in a room, and ideas are born!

    Team Mr Right comprises of entrepreneurs, coders, technology geeks, leaders, innovators and thinkers coming from a variety of locations and different backgrounds with the common aim of bringing a change in the lifestyle of people. Most of us are engineers who decided to break from the cultural shackles, of what typical engineers are expected to do, and create something amazing. Since then, all sorts of crazy things have been happening here. We are here to leave a mark and do something different in our own way.

    Prashant Chaudhary

    An innovator and technology expert Prashant is an alumni of Purdue University USA, and is responsible for company's growth and success since its inception. After over a decade of experiencing lifestyle in the United States, Prashant decided to deliver his technical know-how and life-simplifying ideas in the form of a start-up which we call “Mr. Right”.

    He has diverse experience in technology and business management. Before starting Mr. Right, he helped Fortune 500 clients including Ford, Chrysler, Walmart, in developing multiple business programs for Logistics and Supply Chain Management. When not making day-to-day management decisions, he enjoys coding, playing racquetball, reading Seth Godin, Jason Fried, and Eric Ries!
